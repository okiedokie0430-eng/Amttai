Privacy Notice

Last updated: June 2, 2026

This privacy notice for Amttai ("we," "our," or "us"), describes how and why we might collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your information when you use our services ("Services"), such as when you download and use our mobile application (Amttai), a Private Digital Cookbook & Family Collaboration Utility, or any other application of ours that links to this privacy notice.

Reading this privacy notice will help you understand your privacy rights and choices. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services. If you still have any questions or concerns, please contact us through your registered developer support channel.

Summary of Key Points

This summary provides key points from our privacy notice.

  • What personal information do we process? We may process basic account details provided by you or through Google Sign-In, necessary for authentication and personalizing your private cookbook sandbox.
  • How is your data secured? User-generated custom recipes and interactive shopping lists are secured via individual or team-level permissions. Data is only accessible to the account owner and explicitly invited team members.
  • Who processes your data? Appwrite acts as our primary cloud database, authentication, and hosting processor, ensuring strict compliance with standard data protection rules (GDPR/CCPA frameworks) regarding data deletion and user control.

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you disclose to us

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the Services or create content within your private sandbox.

  • Authentication Data: Profile details returned by Google APIs or direct email registration, including your name, email address, and profile picture URL.
  • Private Sandbox Content: User-generated recipes, images, and interactive shopping lists that you create. These custom recipes are completely private to your individual user account by default.

Application Data Automatically Collected

We automatically collect specific diagnostic and performance telemetry data (Mobile Device Model, OS version, IP address, secure system tokens) technically required to maintain secure server connections and log system bugs.

2. DATA VISIBILITY AND "SHARED KITCHENS"

Amttai operates as a private utility rather than a public social network. Therefore, there are no public broadcasting features, global user profiles, or public feeds.

  • Private by Default: All custom recipes you write, store, and edit are encrypted and completely private to your individual user account.
  • Shared Kitchens (Micro-Communities): You may explicitly invite family members, partners, or roommates to a private "Shared Kitchen" team via Appwrite.
  • Real-Time Syncing: Within a Shared Kitchen, data (custom recipes, modifications, and interactive shopping lists) syncs in real-time between authorized team members via secure WebSockets. This data remains strictly confined to the explicitly invited team members.

3. HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?

We process the personal data collected through our Appwrite backend for operational reasons, including:

  • To facilitate delivery of services: To verify identity, manage your private cookbook sandbox, and facilitate secure real-time WebSocket syncing for Shared Kitchen teams.
  • To protect our Services: To monitor infrastructure security, trace server error logs, and counter unauthorized access.

4. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We disclose your information exclusively to verified third-party service providers who operate as specialized data processors:

  • Appwrite Cloud: Our primary cloud database, authentication, and hosting processor. Appwrite handles backend operations, ensuring compliance with standard data protection rules (GDPR/CCPA frameworks) regarding data deletion and user control.
  • Google OAuth Services: Identity validation gateways verifying your credentials during account registration.

5. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

We preserve your personal records and private sandbox content only for the duration that your user account remains active inside our platform. If you decide to execute an account deletion request, all personal profile elements and custom uploaded data are completely, immediately, and permanently purged from our Appwrite backend servers.

6. HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION SAFE?

User-generated recipes and shopping lists are protected via individual or team-level permissions. Your account telemetry and sandbox data benefit from enterprise-grade transit security (HTTPS/TLS network packet encryption) and automated encryption at rest inside cloud server frames via Appwrite.

7. WHAT ARE YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS?

Regardless of your geographic location, we grant all users an absolute capability to review, download, or fully clear their collected database listings. You can execute a data deletion mandate at any time within the mobile application settings, or by visiting our .

8. HOW CAN YOU CONTACT US?

If you have questions or data removal requests regarding this Privacy Notice, you can reach out via your registered developer support channel or visit our infrastructure landing domain at https://amttai.appwrite.network.
